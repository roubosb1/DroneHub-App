Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: July 20, 2026

You can have your data removed from DroneHub Media systems at any time. This page also serves as the data-deletion instructions required by Meta Platform Terms for apps using Facebook Login.

Disconnect a social account (fastest)

  • In the DroneHub Service, open the connected account and choose Disconnect — this deletes the stored access token immediately.
  • You can also remove "DroneHub App" from your Facebook Business Integrations settings or your Google account permissions. Access ends immediately; any stored tokens are purged on our next cleanup pass.

Request full deletion

Email: app@dronehubmedia.com

Subject: "Data Deletion Request" — include the name and email address on the account. We will confirm receipt, delete personal data and connected-platform data within 30 days, and confirm completion. Records we must keep by law (for example, issued invoices for tax purposes) are retained only as required.

What gets deleted

  • Social platform access tokens and cached analytics for your accounts
  • Portal account credentials and profile information
  • Messages and files associated with your account, where not legally required to be retained
