Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

DroneHub Media ("DroneHub", "we", "us") is a real-estate drone media company operating in Canada and the United States. This policy describes what information the DroneHub operations platform and client portal (the "Service") collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. Questions can always be sent to app@dronehubmedia.com.

Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email address, phone number, company, and login credentials for team members, contractors, and clients invited to the Service.
  • Business records — bookings, quotes, invoices, project files, calendars, and messages created while using the Service.
  • Connected social media data — when you connect a social account (Facebook, Instagram, YouTube), we receive profile information and analytics for that account, such as follower counts, post engagement, reach, and watch time, through the official Meta and Google APIs. We only receive data you (or the account owner) explicitly authorize.
  • Connected calendar data — if you link a calendar feed, we read event titles and times solely to display schedule conflicts to you while booking.
  • Payment information — invoices are processed by Stripe. Card and bank details are handled by Stripe and are never stored on DroneHub systems.

How we use information

  • To operate the Service: scheduling shoots, producing and delivering media, invoicing, messaging, and reporting.
  • To display social media performance analytics to the account owner and to team members the owner has authorized.
  • To communicate about bookings, deliveries, and account matters.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Meta (Facebook & Instagram) platform data

When a Facebook or Instagram account is connected, DroneHub receives an access token and reads profile and insights data through Meta's Graph API. Tokens are stored encrypted on our backend and are used only to retrieve the data described above. Disconnecting an account in the Service, or removing the DroneHub app in your Facebook Business Integrations settings, revokes our access. See our Data Deletion instructions for removing stored data.

YouTube API Services

The Service uses YouTube API Services to display channel and video analytics. By connecting a YouTube channel you agree to the YouTube Terms of Service. Google's handling of your data is described in the Google Privacy Policy. You can revoke DroneHub's access at any time via your Google security settings.

Service providers

We use a small set of infrastructure providers to run the Service: Netlify (hosting), Google Firebase (database), Stripe (payments), Google (Drive, Calendar, YouTube APIs), Meta (Facebook/Instagram APIs), and Filemail (large file transfer). Each receives only the data required to perform its function.

Data retention & deletion

Business records are retained while your account is active and as required for tax and legal purposes. Social media tokens and analytics are deleted when an account is disconnected. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time — see Data Deletion.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with access controls. Access tokens and secrets are kept server-side and are never exposed to other users' browsers.

Children

The Service is a business tool and is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy on this page with an updated effective date.
